MANALI (HIMACHAL PRADESH)

ABOUT

Situated in the lap of the beautiful Himachal Pradesh, Manali is one of those exotic hill stations that has had people's attention captivated since times immemorial. Part of the Kullu district and seated at an altitude of 6727ft, in the peripheries of the Beas river valley, Manali is one of those places where you will find yourself mesmerized by the scenic exquisiteness. The scintillating valleys and snow-peaked mountains, perfectly placed with the foliage and solace that this place will bestow upon you, Manali is like a fairy-tale...
